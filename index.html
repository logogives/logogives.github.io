About Logo Gives

When I was starting out in design, open source projects - whether it was UI kits, psd files or html templates - were extremely helpful in developing the skills I have today.

So with Logo Gives, I can give back to this awesome community which has helped me so much. Use these logos for your digital product, startup, mockups, sports team jersey, coffee mug labels - you name it.

All completely free and open source with no attribution required.
